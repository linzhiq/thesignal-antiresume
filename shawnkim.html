Sangeun (Shawn) Kim is a student at the University of Pennsylvania (C'21) studying Science, Technology, and Society. He was born in Seoul, South Korea, grew up in Queens, NY, and currently resides in Westwood, NJ. He is a transfer student from Franklin & Marshall College in Lancaster, PA. He is passionate about social justice and social impact as he is involved in the UPenn NAACP and Illumna, a nonprofit aimed at mentoring underprivileged students.
